Spanish Rice Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for making one of my favorite dishes; Spanish rice. Its a delicious rice dish that compliments anything and is easily customizable.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (can use up to 1/4 cup)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 cups of medium or long-grain white rice
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • cup of diced fresh or cooked tomatoes
  • Pinch of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Brown the rice: Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium/high heat. Add the rice and stir it so that the rice is lightly coated with the oil. Cook on medium high heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook, stirring frequently another 3 minutes.
  3. Combine broth, tomato, oregano, salt, and browned rice and onions.
  4. Combine the rice and broth mixture. Add the browned rice to the simmering broth
  5. Bring everything to a simmer, cover the pot and lower the heat to barely maintain a low simmer. Cook for 15-25 minutes, depending on the type of rice and the instructions on the rice package
  6. Fluff with a fork or spoon to serve.
